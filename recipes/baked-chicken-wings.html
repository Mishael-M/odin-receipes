Baked Chicken Wings

Description

These baked chicken wings are delicious and can be served with your choice of sides. Usually goes with a rice, chicken gravy, and a vegetable or salad

Ingredients

  • Oil: Olive oil locks in the moisture and gives the seasonings something to stick to.
    • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Seasonings: The baked wings are flavored with a blend of fresh garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
    • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
    • 2 teaspoons chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Wings: Youâ€™ll need 10 chicken wings for this recipe, which makes about two servings.
    • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine. Add chicken wings; reseal and shake to coat. Arrange chicken wings on a baking sheet.
  3. Cook chicken wings in the preheated oven until crisp and cooked through, about 1 hour.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
